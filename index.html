I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Intelligent Infrastructure at Iowa State University, working at the Institute for Transportation. My research transforms complex, real-world datasets — crash records, connected vehicle signals, clinical data — into models that improve safety and inform policy.

Across both transportation and healthcare, I build end-to-end pipelines: from raw data ingestion through feature engineering, modeling with classical ML and transformer architectures, through to interactive dashboards for decision-makers.

My work has been published in peer-reviewed journals, applied in collaboration with state DOTs, and grounded in the belief that good data science serves people, not just models.
